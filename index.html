Parvos Granum

Character

Parvos Granum is a character in the Warframe universe. He is the founder of the group known as the Corpus, a society that values profit above everything else. He was imprisoned for millennia after a failed assasination attempt by his Board of Directors. This led to the corruption of the group, coming up with new forms of "payment" such as "reposessions", which consists in the taking individual's valuables in the form of body parts if they are deemed too inefficient.

Parvos' philosophy (and that of the Corpus previous to their fall) was centered around desire. He shunned contentment, for he saw it as idle or slothfulness.

The 11 Tenets

Throughout the game, the player can collect a series of tablets representing the ideals of the Corpus.

  • "Fear not poverty. Poverty is the bitter soil in which sweet desire blossoms."
  • "Fortune despises the idle man. Stasis is death. Always move forward."
  • "Be envious. Covet. Then take what you desire."
  • "Deception is the sword of wisdom. Be wise."
  • "Beware the idle man who would lull you back into idleness."
  • "Contentment is idleness. Desire inspires action. Nurture all desires."
  • "Shun sentimentality. It is a weakness that binds the idle man."
  • "Fulfill desire and others will follow."
  • "Money begets money."
  • "Charity is power. More charity is more power."
  • "Ours is the grasping golden hand. We are desire. We are Corpus."
